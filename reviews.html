Book of the Week

Five Feet Apart


Now this book is a must-read. A modern retelling of Romeo and Juliet, which I already love for obvious reasons (the adventure, forbidden nature, passionate love so strong they die for each other, what isn't to absolutely love) this book tells the story of two teens torn apart who cannot touch each other due to illness. Their love is forbidden because it could kill them physically, but they chose to see each other anyway. I love how their love begins innocent (I don't like too much inappropriate touching right at the start) but builds up to a beautiful love story despite being forbidden. 5 stars!

Movie of the Week

The Notebook


My pick for this week is a classic- The Notebook. There's so much I love about this movie, from the large romantic gesture Noah makes by climbing on the ferris wheel to the lengths he goes to in order to end up with Ellie. Here is a man who understands how to craft a good letter, he should give Mr. Hervey a lesson! The romance, the hardship, it's everything I love all wrapped into one. On the nights I feel lonely and secluded at this house all on my own, this is the movie I curl up to.
