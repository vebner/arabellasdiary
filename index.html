· About Me ·


Hello everyone, welcome to my website! My name is Arabella, and I'm just a 17 year old girl who has a lot of time on her hands. Here, I'll share my book recommendations (I read a lot), what movie I've been watching during my alone time on the estate, daily crazy adventures I go through and even some romantic tips based on what I've learned from my mother's book collection. Enjoy!

My favorite hobby is reading books, especially ones my mother left me when she died. They're very special to me, and with all my alone time I love to curl up and read those stories again and again! Sometimes I forget I don't live inside them!

I really like to ride horses! My dad is pretty strict but whenever he lets me, I always go out and ride.

Although I do go on plenty of adventures in my books everyday, I want to travel someday. For now, I'll share my crazy adventures I go through right here at home!
